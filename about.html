About this lab

Introduction

Welcome to the Mechanical Lab developed at BNCET Lucknow. The interactive experiment in this lab will give the students an opportunity for learning and better understanding of the basic concepts and constructs of Tube Boiler.

Explore this lab

This lab covers 10 different concepts in C programming, like Numerical Approximation, Functions, Advanced Control Flow, Arrays, Structures, Basic Control Flow, Strings, Pointers, Recursion, Expression Evaluation with simulations. Please go ahead and explore this lab on your own and play simulations. link to the lab

Prerequisites and References

This lab doesn't need any third party non FOSS (plugin or software) to run simulations. Javascript needs to be enabled on your browser.
